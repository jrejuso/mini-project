Clients Testimonials

Monkey D. Luffy @luffysenpai

I recently had the pleasure of taking a seafood trip in the Kwebang Lampas, and I must say, it was an adventure of a lifetime. From the bustling seafood markets of Pagbilao to the pristine beaches of Isla Grande, I was blown away by the variety and quality of the seafood on offer.

Roronoa Zoro @zoronoju

One highlight of my trip was a seafood feast at a local restaurant in Isla Grande. The restaurant had an impressive selection of fresh seafood, including crab, prawns, squid, and several types of fish. The grilled squid and buttered garlic shrimp were particularly outstanding and left me wanting more.

Nico Robin @nicorobin

I visited the seafood markets in Pugad Lawin Resort, where I found a wide variety of seafood at affordable prices. The Pagbilao Seafood Market in Quezon was a definite highlight, where I could purchase fresh seafood and have it cooked to my liking at one of the many restaurants in the market.

Vinsmoke Sanji @sanjutoru

Overall, I highly recommend a seafood trip in the Isla Grande Pagbilao. The flavors of the fresh seafood are unmatched, and the experience is truly unforgettable. Don't be afraid to try different cooking styles, such as grilled, fried, and steamed, to fully appreciate the flavors of the seafood. I can't wait to return and taste more of what the Philippines have to offer.
