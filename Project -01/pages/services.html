Things To Do In Isla Grande Pagbilao

Beach Camping

Reviews Book A Travel
Nipa Hut Of Titan Beach Resort

For those looking for a more off-the-beaten-path experience, there are several remote beaches in the Isla Grande that offer beach camping. One such island is Balete beach, located in Sitio Tubahin. The place is only accessible by walk and offers a tranquil setting with its unspoiled beaches and crystal-clear waters.

Tent Fitcheng Of Tagong Paraiso Beach Resort

Another great option for beach camping is Tagong Paraiso Beach Resort, known for its white sand beaches and vibrant nightlife. The island has several beachfront campsites that offer basic amenities such as showers and restrooms. The campsites are situated in secluded spots, providing a sense of privacy and serenity.

Bonfire And Friends Are Perfect Combination

One of the most popular destinations for beach camping is Titan beach Resort, where you can camp on the white sand beaches of Isla Grande. The beaches offer stunning views of the surrounding limestone cliffs and turquoise waters, making it the perfect place to relax and unwind.

Relaxing Bonfire

Beach camping in the Isla Grande is an experience like no other. With its crystal clear waters, breathtaking sunsets, and pristine beaches, the Isla Grande offers some of the best beach camping spots in the province of Quezon.

Island Hopping

Reviews Book A Travel
Cave Entrance Of Kwebang Lampas

Kwebang Lampas is a hidden gem in the Pagbilao, with its pristine waters, limestone cliffs, and rich marine life. Visitors can take an island-hopping tour of the island, exploring cave, swimming in crystal-clear waters, and discovering the local culture and traditions.

Crystal Clear Water Of Bilaran Sandbar

Bilaran Sandbar is a popular destination for island hopping, with its pristine waters and stunning sands. Visitors can take a tour of the region, hopping from one island to another, exploring hidden coves and beaches, and experiencing the local culture and cuisine.

Aerial View of Pugad Lawin Resort

Pugad Lawin Resort is a relaxing paradise, but it's also an ideal destination for island hopping. Visitors can take a tour of the region, visiting nearby beaches like La-Isla Salamin and Kwebang Lampas, swimming in crystal-clear waters, and enjoying the laid-back island vibe.

Rock Formation Of Titan Beach Resort

Titan Beach is a hidden gem in the Pagbilao Quezon, with its pristine waters, limestone cliffs, and rich marine life. Visitors can take an island-hopping tour of the region, exploring hidden lagoons, swimming in crystal-clear waters, and discovering the local culture and traditions.

Swimming At La-Isla Salamin

La Isla Salamin is known for its beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters, making it a popular destination for swimming and snorkeling. Visitors can explore underwater caves, swim with starfish.

Snorkling in Kawayanin Beach

Kawayanin Beaach is a picturesque beach nestled in the Pagbilao Quezon. Visitors can swim in the clear, blue waters while enjoying views of the surrounding mountains and charming villages.

Pagbilao Grande Island Marine Sanctuary

The Grande Island Reef is one of the coral reef system and is home to an incredible array of marine life. Visitors can explore the reef on a guided tour or take a dip in the warm, clear waters for an unforgettable swimming experience.

Bilaran Sandbar

Bilaran SandBar is a popular destination for swimming and water sports, with its crystal-clear waters and stunning starfish. Visitors can swim, paddleboard, or kayak in the sea, or take a scenic sun bathing along the shore for breathtaking views.
